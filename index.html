About CareMED

CareMED is a robust, modular Hospital Information Management System (HMIS) built by Fintech Innovation Ke. Designed for the realities of African healthcare, it enables hospitals and clinics to streamline operations, reduce fraud, and improve patient care. Fully web-based, it works on Linux and Windows, supporting both local and cloud deployments.

Key Features

  • Outpatient & Inpatient Management
  • Integrated Billing & Cashiering
  • Pharmacy, Lab, and Radiology Modules
  • Accounts Receivable & Payable
  • Inventory, Procurement & Asset Management
  • Electronic Medical Records (EMR)
  • Business Intelligence (Power BI Ready)
  • Cloud or On-Premise Hosting
  • Multi-location support via browser access
  • Security & Role-Based Access Control

Proven in the Field

CareMED is already deployed in several large missionary hospitals in Kenya. It is tailored for scalability, low total cost of ownership, and minimal IT overhead.
