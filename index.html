About CareMED

CareMED is a powerful and flexible Hospital Information Management System developed by Fintech Innovation Ke. It addresses the unique challenges faced by hospitals and clinics across Africa, integrating departments, digitizing records, and giving management deep control over operations and finances.

Core Modules

  • Patient Registration & Queue Management
  • OPD, IPD, Billing, Discharge
  • Pharmacy, Laboratory, Radiology
  • Accounts Receivable/Payable, Trial Balance
  • Inventory, Procurement & Asset Management
  • MOH Reporting, Payroll, Messaging
  • Business Intelligence Dashboards

Why Hospitals Choose CareMED

  • End-to-end digitization of clinical and financial processes
  • Prevents fraud and financial leakage
  • Real-time reporting and Power BI-ready dashboards
  • Browser-based: No installation needed on PCs
  • Role-based access and strong audit trail
  • Low cost of ownership with Linux and open-source tech

System Architecture

CareMED is a fully browser-based platform. Staff access the system using Chrome or Edge, eliminating the need for individual installations. The server infrastructure supports both local and cloud hosting, enabling multi-site deployments with secure VPN access for remote support and backups.
